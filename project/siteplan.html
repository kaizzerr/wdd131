Site Plan: Experience Calgary

Site Name

Experience Calgary

The site's name will be Experience Calgary as it serves as a preview for what the site offers, while also making it easy to remember and look up.

Optional domain availability: experience-calgary.org

Site Purpose

To help tourists in Calgary

This site aims to be a guide that provides different places that one might want to visit when in Calgary

Scenarios

- What are the places to visit in and around Calgary?

- Are there any events to keep an eye out for?

Color Schema

I selected red, grey, white, and black since that is the color of Calgary. The red is used for the main header, the grey is for the footer, the white is the background color, and the black is for the main paragraph's colors.

Typography

I selected Merriweather for the Headings since it provokes a sense of elegantness, while also using Rubik for the paragraphs and body to feel casual for the user
